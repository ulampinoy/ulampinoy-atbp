Slow cooked beef stew with vegetables

### INGREDIENTS
* 1 big fish cut into 3 pieces
* 3 large tomatoes
* 3 eggs
* 1 medium onion
* 5 gloves garlic
* 3 tbsp patis (fish sauce)
* 3 cups of water
* 1 siling berde (green chili)
* black pepper
* spring onion as garnish
