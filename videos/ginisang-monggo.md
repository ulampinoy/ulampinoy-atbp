Mung beans cooked with pork belly strips, bitter gourd leaves

### INGREDIENTS
* 250 grams monggo (mung beans)
* 1/4 kg. pork belly, cut in to chunks
* 3 gloves garlic
* 1 big onion
* 2 medium tomatoes
* ampalaya leaves
* patis
* cooking oil
* 1.5 liters of water (use for pre-boiling the beans)
