The default pancit.

### INGREDIENTS:
* 1/2 piece dressed chicken (boiled, deboned and shredded; the stock use for cooking the noodles)
* 1/2 kilo chicken liver
* 250 grams bihon (rice noodles)
* 1 medium carrots
* 100 grams green beans
* 2 stalks celery
* 1/2 cup soy sauce
* 3 tbsp oyster sauce
* black pepper
* cooking oil
