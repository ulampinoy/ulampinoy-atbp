Crispy, golden and garlicky pork belly

### INGREDIENTS:
* 1 kg pork belly (cut into thick strips)
* 1 head of garlic (cruched, peeled and chooped)
* 1/2 cup vinegar
* 1/2 cup water
* salt and black pepper to taste
